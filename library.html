Hult Prize 2023

Sustainable Energy Solutions

Powering the Future: A Year of Innovation

Competition Period: September 2022 - August 2023 45 Teams Participated

The 2023 Hult Prize season at Nile University focused on sustainable energy solutions, challenging students to develop innovative approaches to the global energy crisis. Our campus competition saw unprecedented participation with 45 teams presenting groundbreaking solutions.

The winning team, "SolarTech Innovators," developed a revolutionary solar panel efficiency system that increases energy output by 35% while reducing manufacturing costs. Their solution caught the attention of international judges and secured them a spot in the regional finals.

Key Highlights:

  • Record-breaking 45 team participation
  • 3 teams advanced to regional finals
  • $50,000 in total prize money distributed
  • 2 startups launched post-competition
  • International media coverage in 5 countries

Hult Prize 2022

Food Security & Nutrition

Feeding the Future: Innovative Solutions for Global Hunger

Competition Period: September 2021 - August 2022 38 Teams Participated

The 2022 season marked Nile University's second year participating in the Hult Prize, with a focus on addressing food security and nutrition challenges. Students developed innovative solutions ranging from vertical farming systems to nutrition-tracking mobile applications.

Team "NutriGrow" emerged as campus champions with their hydroponic farming system designed for urban environments in developing countries. Their solution demonstrated a 300% increase in crop yield while using 90% less water than traditional farming methods.

Key Highlights:

  • 38 diverse teams from 12 different faculties
  • First regional finalist from Nile University
  • Partnership established with local NGOs
  • 3 pilot projects implemented in local communities
  • Featured in Egyptian national television
