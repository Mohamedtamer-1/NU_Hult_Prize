Hult Prize 2024: Climate Action Challenge

Open for Registration +

Challenge Overview

Address climate change while creating sustainable livelihoods for 10,000 people by 2030. Solutions should focus on renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, or climate adaptation strategies.

Timeline

  • Registration Opens: January 15, 2024
  • Registration Closes: March 15, 2024
  • Campus Competition: April 20, 2024
  • Regional Finals: June 15, 2024
  • Global Finals: September 2024

Prizes

  • Global Winner: $1,000,000
  • Regional Winners: $25,000 each
  • Campus Winners: $5,000
  • Mentorship: 6-month accelerator program

Eligibility

  • Current university students (undergraduate or graduate)
  • Teams of 3-4 members
  • At least one team member must be from Nile University
  • Age limit: 18-29 years

Hult Prize OnCampus Program

Ongoing +

Program Overview

Year-round program designed to build entrepreneurial skills and develop social ventures. Includes workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities.

Program Structure

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Weekly Workshops: Tuesdays 6-8 PM
  • Mentorship Sessions: Bi-weekly
  • Demo Day: End of semester

Benefits

  • Access to industry mentors
  • Networking with successful entrepreneurs
  • Seed funding opportunities
  • Certificate of completion

Requirements

  • Commitment to attend 80% of sessions
  • Develop a viable business plan
  • Present at Demo Day
  • Participate in peer feedback sessions
